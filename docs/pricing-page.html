Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a 'project'?
A project is any AI application or website that you want to monitor and keep alive. Each unique domain or subdomain counts as a separate project.
How does the free trial work?
You get full access to all features of your chosen plan for 30 days. No credit card required. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime during the trial.
What if I need more projects than my plan allows?
On Growth plan, you can add additional projects for $5/month each. Enterprise plan includes unlimited projects.
How often does VibeSecure perform keep-alives?
By default, we perform keep-alives every 15 minutes. On Growth and higher plans, you can set custom intervals. Additional charges apply for intervals shorter than 15 minutes.
What happens if an update fails?
Our automated rollback system immediately reverts to the last stable version. You'll receive a notification about the failure and rollback status.
